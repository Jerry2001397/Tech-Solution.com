Section 1: Introduction

01

Tech Bridge Liberia -TBL is committed to fostering a workplace that promotes ethical conduct, professionalism, and legal compliance across all operations. This policy document outlines the guidelines and expectations for all employees, contractors, partners, third-party service providers, and stakeholders affiliated with Tech Bridge Liberia -TBL.

Given the diverse industries we serve—including business, health, technology, education, finance, and more—these policies are designed to ensure consistent standards of behavior and performance, regardless of the specific sector.

Section 2: Scope

02

This document applies to all Tech Bridge Liberia -TBL employees, contractors, consultants, and third-party service providers. It is binding in all professional interactions on company premises and during remote and on-site work.

  • All employees and contractors
  • Third-party service providers
  • Company premises and remote work environments
  • All professional interactions

Section 3: Legal Compliance

03

Tech Bridge Liberia -TBL is committed to adhering to all applicable laws and regulations in every jurisdiction where we operate.

3.1 Data Protection Regulations

All employees must ensure that any data handling, storage, and transmission comply with applicable Data Protection regulations at an assigned project.

3.2 Industry-Specific Regulations

Understanding and compliance with industry-specific laws (e.g., health, mining, finance) that affect the data we collect and manage.

3.3 Intellectual Property

Respect for intellectual property rights, including the prohibition of unauthorized use, copying, or distribution of software, trademarks, or confidential information.

3.4 Anti-Corruption and Bribery

Zero tolerance for corruption or bribery in any form. Employees must not offer, give, or receive any form of bribe or unethical inducement.

3.5 Non-Disclosure

Adherence to strict confidentiality agreements to protect company and client data, proprietary information, and trade secrets.

3.6 Contracts & Materials

All contracts given to Tech Bridge Liberia -TBL must be fully handled by the company without third-party intervention unless approved. Materials and tools needed for contracts should not be purchased without approval from our technicians. Tech Bridge Liberia -TBL is not responsible for damage to unapproved-purchased materials.

Section 4: Code of Conduct

04

All employees and associated personnel are expected to conduct themselves professionally and ethically, adhering to the following principles:

Integrity

Demonstrating honesty and strong moral principles in all dealings, both within the company and externally.

Respect

Treating colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with respect, dignity, and fairness.

Accountability

Taking responsibility for one's actions and the consequences thereof.

Professionalism

Upholding high standards of work, including punctuality, reliability, and competence.

Compliance

Adhering to all company policies, including those related to health, safety, diversity, and harassment prevention.

Section 5: Remote Work Guidelines

05

5.1 Secure Connections

Employees and contractors must use secure, company-approved hardware and software when executing tasks remotely.

5.2 Work Environment

Remote work environments should be conducive to professional work, free from distractions, and secure against unauthorized access.

5.3 Communication

Regular communication with supervisors and team members is required. Employees should be reachable during standard work hours.

5.4 Confidentiality

The same standards of confidentiality apply when working remotely as they do when on company premises.

Section 6: Penalties for Non-Compliance

06

Violations of company policies may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract.

Minor Infractions

  • Verbal or written warnings
  • Additional training requirements

Serious Violations

  • Suspension
  • Demotion
  • Mandatory leave without pay

Severe Breaches

  • Immediate termination
  • Legal action
  • Referral to law enforcement

Section 7: Conduct on Company Premises

07

Employees are expected to adhere to the following conduct guidelines while on company premises:

7.1 Dress Code

Professional or company-prescribed attire is required at all working times. Casual wear may be permitted on designated days with prior approval.

7.2 Workplace Etiquette

Courteous behavior, respect for personal space, and proper use of company resources are mandatory.

7.3 Substance Use Policy

The use of alcohol or illegal drugs on company premises or during work hours is strictly prohibited.

Section 8: Ethical Considerations

08

Tech Bridge Liberia -TBL operates under a robust ethical framework that goes beyond legal compliance. We believe in creating a sustainable and positive impact on our stakeholders and communities.

  • Environmental responsibility in all operations
  • Fair treatment of all employees and contractors
  • Transparency in business practices
  • Community engagement and social responsibility

Policy Acknowledgment

By engaging with Tech Bridge Liberia-TBL in any capacity, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with all policies outlined in this document. Questions regarding this policy should be directed to our Human Resources or Management team.
